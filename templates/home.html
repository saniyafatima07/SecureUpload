SecureUpload

SecureUpload is a powerful encryption tool that uses advanced cryptographic principles to ensure your files remain completely private. The encryption process happens entirely in your browser - it never stores your files or passphrases.


Security Features

  • Uses AES-256 encryption with Fernet (symmetric authenticated cryptography)
  • Implements secure Base64 encoding for key generation
  • Employs PBKDF2 key derivation with 100,000 iterations to protect against brute force attacks
  • Generates a unique salt (random bytes) for each encryption to ensure security

Encryption Process

Upload your file and enter a strong passphrase
Unique salt is generated for your key
File encrypted with Fernet/AES
Salt prepended to encrypted file
Download your secured .enc file
